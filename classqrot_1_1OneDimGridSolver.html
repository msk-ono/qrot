Detailed Description

Solve one-dimensional grid-problems defined in 1403.2975.

Implementation of section 4 of 1403.2975.

Find solutions a + \sqrt{2} b \in Z2 such that next two inequalities hold:

  • x0 <= a + \sqrt{2} b <= x1
  • y0 <= a - \sqrt{2} b <= y1
